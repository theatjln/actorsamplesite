About Me

I am just a young dreamer who is always fascinated about how movies and TV series are made. Although most of my younger and vulnerable years I've spent on being a singer and a programmer, I’ve always loved watching films and actors on screen. They give such great impact to people by sharing great messages and morals in each of their shows and projects. Read more..

I myself started to be inspired to pursue acting when I was a child. I was given a chance to represent my school in an inter-school dramatic dialogue contest. From there on, I have never stopped dreaming to have a full-time career in this field. Being an actor means putting yourself in somebody else’s shoes and understand that person in a way more deeper level. And because of that, you become less judgmental and more empathetic to every person you get to meet and interact with. Because every person has a backstory and someone is the way he or she is because of so many factors. Acting professionally for me makes me a better person. For every role or character I have to portray as an actor, I get to have the chance to grow by adapting someone else’s life in a rapid and technical way as possible. Actors should be really good in their craft in order to deliver the exact message a story has to tell.

This is why acting is a difficult job. It requires skills, dedication, determination, perserverance, and the right attitude and mindset in order to sustain being an actor. Some actors might look good on the outside but acting is a very tiresome work. You need to be wise so you could sustain yourself and stay in the industry and business. ...hide

Biography

Born and raised in a simple yet loving family, Liz De Guzman is among the three children of Des and Pepot De Guzman. Growing up, she had always had her eyes and heart in arts and technology.

As a student, Liz is very diligent and involved. She graduated first honor in kindergarten and got the 'Artist of the Year' awardee with an honorable mention in elementary. A choir member, school paper's feature writer, she thrived as an achiever in her early years. She have won various competitions in theatre, chorale, postermaking, and floral design both in school and outside the school.

During high school, she was the president of the student body and vice president for her region in the national federation of student governments. She was also among the ten high school students who received leadership and academic awards from their country's vice president.

The summer before her college, she spent her time working in a music studio and doing side gigs as model, background actor, and singer. She had a professional theatre experience during the start of her college and after that, she had more job offers in singing, acting, and modeling. But she didn't had the chance to pursue them as career because she focused in her studies. She was an academic scholar graduate in a state university with a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science. After college, she had a professional working experience as a lead web developer and software development operations manager in a technology company.
